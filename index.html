About Me

I am a dedicated AI Engineer and Researcher with a strong academic background in Artificial Intelligence from Pak-Austria Fachhochschule Institute of Applied Sciences and Technology. My expertise spans Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing, Edge AI, Federated Learning, and Secure ML Systems.

As an AI Research Intern at AITec, I co-authored an IJCNN 2025 paper on Edge-Enabled Federated Learning and developed secure aggregation protocols. My current role as a full-time AI Researcher at AITec involves leading research on secure aggregation in federated learning and developing custom single-object tracking systems for real-world deployment on Jetson Nano. I am also preparing to present an ICSBC 2025 paper on encrypted model aggregation.

My passion lies in creating intelligent solutions and contributing to impactful research, with a focus on privacy-preserving systems and optimizing AI for distributed and resource-constrained environments.
